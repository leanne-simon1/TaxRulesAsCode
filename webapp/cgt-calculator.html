Capital gains tax: current rules vs the 2027 reform

Enter a disposal — past or forecast — and the rules engine returns the CGT cost under today's rules (50% discount), under the new rules that commence 1 July 2027 (indexation + a 30% minimum tax), and your actual grandfathered outcome given the dates. Every number, and its legislative reference, comes live from openfisca_rules/variables/cgt.py — this page only asks and displays.

Forecasting: acquisition and sale dates can be in the future. If you buy and sell after 1 July 2027 the new rules apply in full; sell before and the current rules apply; hold across that date and the gain is grandfathered — split between the two regimes.
