Negative gearing: current law vs the 2027 quarantine

Four views over one ruleset. From the 2027-28 income year, net rental losses on a residential dwelling acquired on or after 7:30pm AEST 12 May 2026 are quarantined — usable only against other rental income or residential capital gains, otherwise carried forward. Everything below is computed live by the rules engine from Negative Gearing Ruleset.md; this page only asks and displays.

Enacted, not yet commenced: the quarantine (Treasury Laws Amendment (Tax Reform No. 1) Act 2026, Schedule 2 — new s 26-155 ITAA 1997) applies to losses incurred in the 2027-28 income year and later. Interests acquired before the Budget announcement are grandfathered; new residential dwellings are exempt.
